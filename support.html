Frequently asked questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Summa subscriptions are billed and managed by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android). To cancel:

  • iPhone or iPad: Open Settings, tap your name at the top, select Subscriptions, find Summa, and tap Cancel Subscription.
  • Android: Open the Google Play Store app, tap your profile icon, go to Payments & subscriptions, then Subscriptions, find Summa, and tap Cancel subscription.

You will keep Premium access until the end of your current billing period.

Where is my data stored?

All your financial data lives only on your device. Summa has no servers, no accounts, and no cloud sync. Your income, expenses, goals, and spending log never leave your phone.

Will I lose my data if I delete the app?

Yes. Uninstalling Summa removes the local data permanently. Your active subscription is unaffected and can be restored on reinstall via the Restore Purchases button on the paywall.

Restore Purchases is not working

A few things to check:

  • Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID or Google account that originally purchased the subscription.
  • Wait a minute and try again. Apple and Google sometimes take a moment to confirm the restore.
  • Force-close the app and reopen. Summa auto-syncs your subscription state on every launch.
  • If still not working, email us with your purchase date and the email associated with your Apple ID or Google account.

Is Summa financial advice?

No. Summa is a personal planning tool, not a financial advisor. Projections, recommendations, and the safe-to-spend number are estimates based on what you enter and general financial assumptions. Consult a qualified professional before making major financial decisions.

I have a feature request or bug report

Send it to support.summa.app@gmail.com. We genuinely read everything, even if we cannot reply to every message. Bug reports get faster attention when they include your iPhone or Android model and what you were doing when the issue happened.
